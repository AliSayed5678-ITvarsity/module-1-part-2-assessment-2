THE MASK

Main actors:

  • - Jim Carrey as Stanley Ipkiss / The Mask
  • - Cameron Diaz as Tina Carlyle
  • - Peter Riegert as Lt. Mitch Kellaway
  • - Peter Greene as Dorian Tyrel
  • - Amy Yasbeck as Peggy Brandt

AGE RESTRICTION: PG-13

SHOWING TIMES :

  • 8:00 - 10:00
  • 14:00 - 16:00

Stanley Ipkiss (Jim Carrey), a timid bank clerk, discovers a mysterious mask that transforms him into a zany trickster with reality-bending powers. This alter ego, known as “The Mask”, allows Stanley to act out his wildest dreams and desires, including wooing the beautiful singer Tina Carlyle (Cameron Diaz).

However, the mask’s power also draws the attention of the local crime boss, Dorian Tyrell (Peter Greene), who has his own nefarious plans. As Stanley navigates his dual identity, he must contend with the police, the mob, and his own chaotic antics.
