MILKY LANE


A Brief on us :Milky Lane is South Africa’s beloved dessert destination, serving up smiles since 1958 with its iconic soft serve ice cream and delightful treats. Known for its wonderfilled creations, Milky Lane offers a variety of ice cream desserts, waffles, and shakes, all made with their secret NiceCream recipe. The brand’s cheerful atmosphere and quality products have made it a go-to spot for sweet indulgences and joyful experiences
