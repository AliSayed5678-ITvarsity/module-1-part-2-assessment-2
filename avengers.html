When the Asgardian Loki (Tom Hiddleston) arrives on Earth with the intent to rule humanity, S.H.I.E.L.D. director Nick Fury (Samuel L. Jackson) is compelled to activate the Avengers Initiative. This initiative brings together a group of extraordinary individuals: Tony Stark/Iron Man (Robert Downey Jr.), Steve Rogers/Captain America (Chris Evans), Bruce Banner/The Hulk (Mark Ruffalo), Thor (Chris Hemsworth), Natasha Romanoff/Black Widow (Scarlett Johansson), and Clint Barton/Hawkeye (Jeremy Renner).

Together, these heroes face the challenge of stopping Loki and his alien army, the Chitauri, from subjugating Earth. The film follows their struggles to work as a team and their eventual unity to defend the planet from the impending invasion.
