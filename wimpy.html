WIMPY


A Brief on us : Wimpy is a much-loved fast-food chain in South Africa, renowned for its warm hospitality and hearty meals. Established in 1967 in Durban, Wimpy has grown to become a staple of South African dining, known for its family-friendly atmosphere and a menu that caters to a variety of tastes. With a rich history of over 50 years, Wimpy has become synonymous with delicious breakfasts, satisfying lunches, and indulgent milkshakes.
