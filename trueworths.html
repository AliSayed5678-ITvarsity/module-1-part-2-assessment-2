TRUEWORTHS


A Brief on us :Truworths International Ltd. is a leading fashion retailer based in Cape Town, South Africa, with a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1917. As part of a group that operates primarily in South Africa and the United Kingdom, Truworths offers a wide range of fashion clothing, footwear, homeware, and related merchandise. The company is known for its superior quality and aspirational fashion, with a brand portfolio that includes Truworths Man, Inwear, Identity, Daniel Hechter, LTD, Ginger Mary, Uzzi, Hey Betty, Earthaddict, Earthchild, Naartjie, Office London, Loads of Living, and Context
