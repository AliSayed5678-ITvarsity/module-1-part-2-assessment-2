PICK N PAY


A Brief on us : Pick n Pay is a prominent South African retailer known for its wide range of products including food, clothing, and general merchandise. Founded in 1967 by Raymond Ackerman, the company has grown to become a key player in the retail market, operating various store formats such as hypermarkets, supermarkets, and convenience stores. With a mission to serve customers with heart and create an excellent shopping experience, Pick n Pay upholds values of customer passion, respect, personal growth, leadership, integrity, community support, and accountability
