A brief on us : Ocean Basket is South Africa’s favorite seafood restaurant, renowned for its Mediterranean spirit of generosity and commitment to delicious seafood. Founded in 1995 in Pretoria, the brand has grown to over 200 restaurants in 16 countries. Inspired by the founders’ mother, Mama Liza, Ocean Basket’s menu invites customers to experience the fabulous flavors of the ocean with friends and family
